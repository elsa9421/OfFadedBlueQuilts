On a Quest for Consistency and bad Grammar

I woke up this morning only to spend over an hour in bed scrolling through a combination of irrelevancy and narcissism (aka Instagram). I did find a gem in there, though. I have a screenshot of it saved on my phone. I could, obviously go to the room, get my phone, look through it and obtain said screenshot but – will I?

The answer is, of course, obvious and a metaphor for how I seem to be handling my life right now – a lot of very easily researchable and answerable questions left void.

Where was I? The quote – yes. It was about consistency. “Consistency is about waking up every day and doing the same thing until it becomes a part of your very being. And that is what adulting is all about” (Oh? Adulting isn’t a word? Or so MS Word informs me).

So here I am, some six hours and half of a bag of Flamin’ Hot Lays later deciding that I will make a conscious effort to be consistent, follow up, and be all round better. No, there isn’t going to be a noun after better – stop looking for it! I just want to be better fullstop – skipping punctuation too. Wow. Rebel, much?

Which reminds me I have a meeting in five and tax forms to figure out.

Ta-ta!
