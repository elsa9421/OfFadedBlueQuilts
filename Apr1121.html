April 11th, 2021

An Act of God.

Hey there!
Look at me breaking the fourth wall with the very first sentence. How (what's the word ?) forward of me. The point of this piece here is to acknowledge a miraculous something that happened to me. It's important to document, I'm told.
I am here because I was always always supposed to be here. This is an (as Anne of Green Gables would say) epoch in my life. Will I ever be able to look at anything, if not with rose-tinted shades? For if everything that has ever happened was meant to be, can anything even if incredibly awful have any affect? Oh, death where is thy sting?
* cough cough * Er.
If I'm not careful I'm going to wake up an optimist or worse, that dog (Mr. Sunshine?) from BoJack Horseman.


Let me try and articulate.

I'm cynical, clinical, and somehow still whimsical,
I'm more or less here or there,
I'm in shock, I want to cry but I've long since forgotten how -
I'm happy, I'm sad, I'm anxious, I'm excited.
I'm tired but sleepless so I tire myself out to sleep.
But mostly, I'm just grateful.

PLACEHOLDER + REMINDER to future me : Miracles happen. They are real and true and magical.


If it can happen to me, the very least of things, it can happen for you.
Hang on.
It's going to be a bumpy ride.
